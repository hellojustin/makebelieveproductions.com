# Claude Homepage

## What This Is

A personal homepage for Justin's software consulting company.

## The Dual Audience

This site needs to speak to two things at once:

- **Engineering credibility** — clients need to trust that Justin can build serious, production-quality software products
- **Artistic sensibility** — the work has an aesthetic dimension; many clients are artists, and the products built for them are used by everyday consumers

The tone and design should feel like it was made by someone who takes both craft and aesthetics seriously — not a generic dev-for-hire shop.

## The Clients

Clients are typically **artists and creators** who are building software products for consumer audiences. They're not usually technical, but they have strong creative vision. Justin's role is to translate that vision into working software.

## Reference Materials

Non-code reference files (inspiration, brand notes, copy examples, image references) live in `.claude/reference/`. Check there when working on design or copy decisions.

## Notes for Claude

- When suggesting copy, aim for language that is confident but not corporate — human, specific, a little warm
- When suggesting design direction, lean toward considered and minimal rather than flashy
- This is a homepage, not a portfolio dump — focus on communicating identity and trust quickly
