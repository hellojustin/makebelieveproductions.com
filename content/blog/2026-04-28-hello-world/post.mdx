We've added a writing space to the site. It's a small thing, but it's been a long time coming.

## Why a blog

Most of the work we do at Make Believe Productions happens behind closed doors —
under NDAs, in private repos, inside companies that aren't ready to share what
they're building. The interesting part of the work, though, is rarely the
specific product. It's the way we got there: the conversations, the trade-offs,
the small decisions that turned out to matter more than they looked.

This is the place for that.

## What to expect here

A few categories I think about regularly:

- **Notes from the studio.** Specific tactics, working patterns, things that have
  earned their keep across multiple engagements.
- **Conversations about taste.** The edges where craft and aesthetics intersect
  with engineering — a place I think about a lot.
- **Tools we use and tools we wish existed.** With a bias toward small, sharp
  things you can actually pick up and use.
